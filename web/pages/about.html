Hongry.com's Mission Statement

Hongry.com's mission is offer outstanding mobile technology to restaurants, cafes, and entertainment establishments; to provide this mobile technology to help them establish a closer relationship with their clients; to provide this mobile technology at a reasonable price; to be the vanguard of mobile technology; and to provide a high-quality service that meets all of our partners' demands
